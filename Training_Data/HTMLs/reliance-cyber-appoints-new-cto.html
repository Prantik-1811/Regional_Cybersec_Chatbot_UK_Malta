With over two decades of experience in technology and cybersecurity, Munro is perfectly positioned to lead Reliance Cyber’s innovation and expansion initiatives. An expert in shaping strategic direction within the tech and cybersecurity sectors, Munro has held leadership positions at organisations such as KPMG and NCC Group. He has also served on the board of CREST and acted as an expert advisor for Cybersecurity to the UK government. Bringing a wealth of knowledge and a proven track record of success, Munro’s expertise will be crucial for advancing Reliance Cyber’s mission to deliver industry-leading cybersecurity services. He will focus on driving innovation and ensuring exceptional customer experiences. “ We are excited to welcome Lawrence Munro to our team,” said Mark Gascoigne, MD of Reliance Cyber. “His extensive background in cybersecurity and proven leadership skills are exactly what Reliance Cyber needs as we continue to grow and innovate in this dynamic industry. Lawrence’s vision for the future of cybersecurity aligns perfectly with our goals to lead the market in securing enterprises internationally .” Munro commented “I’m honoured and excited to join Reliance Cyber as their CTO. I’ve long admired their technical capabilities and firm focus on clients. I believe that I’m joining at an exciting moment in the company’s history, where we’re poised to make excellent contributions to U.K. cybersecurity.” About Reliance Cyber Since our founding in 2003, Reliance Cyber has established itself as a leading Managed Security Service Provider (MSSP). By combining deep cybersecurity expertise with a true partnership ethos, we enable organisations to concentrate on their core business. Working closely with customers to understand their challenges and problems, we use our teams, our own IP and our technology partners to put together a solution which is specifically built to address the situation and help identify, understand and manage business risk associated to cyber security. We are proud to provide independent cyber security services to organisations in a range of sectors, defending against threats of all kinds, including at the nation-state-level, and we’re always ready for our next challenge.
