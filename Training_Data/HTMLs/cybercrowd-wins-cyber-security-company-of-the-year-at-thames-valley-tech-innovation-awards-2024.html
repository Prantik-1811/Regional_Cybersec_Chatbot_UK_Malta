https://www.cybercrowd.co.uk/news/cybercrowd-wins-cyber-security-company-of-the-year-at-thames-valley-tech-innovation-awards-2024 News Reading, Berkshire – July 19, 2024 – CyberCrowd is delighted to announce its win as ‘Cyber Security Company of the Year’ at the Thames Valley Tech & Innovation Awards 2024. The gala event, held at Reading’s Select Car Leasing Stadium, celebrated the region’s strongest tech talent and innovation. Kris Reddin, Sales Team Lead at CyberCrowd, expressed the team’s excitement: “We are thrilled to win this award. This year has been a period of tremendous growth for us. We’ve expanded our team with apprentices, graduates, and senior professionals, enhancing our capabilities as a business and enabling us to better serve our customers. Our range of cybersecurity services is designed to protect critical digital infrastructures. With the launch of our on-site 24/7/365 Security Operations Centre, our customers now benefit from enhanced real-time monitoring and quicker response times, ensuring their data and operations are more secure than ever. Our commitment to client support and delivering the highest level of service remains our top priority.” This win underscores CyberCrowd’s unwavering dedication to our clients, as we continue to provide the most advanced cybersecurity services and information. Ensuring robust cybersecurity is vital in today’s digital landscape, and we are committed to staying at the forefront of the industry to protect our clients’ interests. The sixth annual Thames Valley Tech & Innovation Awards highlighted the outstanding achievements of leading businesses across the region. Finalists represented a diverse array of fields, including software development, cybersecurity, hardware supply and manufacturing, cloud solutions, science-based industries, artificial intelligence, and space technology. Comedian Simon Evans hosted the Awards with his trademark wit, making the evening both entertaining and memorable. Richard Thompson, Chief Executive at The Business Magazine, commented, “We had many, many excellent entries, and I’d like to thank the judges who interviewed a number of the shortlisted companies and diligently assessed the strengths and potential of the contenders.” We extend our congratulations to all the shortlisted companies and winners. A special thank you to all the sponsors, especially Penningtons Manches Cooper , and to The Business Magazine for hosting a fantastic evening. CyberCrowd had a brilliant time celebrating with everyone. For more information about CyberCrowd and our award-winning cybersecurity services, please visit our website or contact us directly. About CyberCrowd: CyberCrowd is a leading cybersecurity and information services provider dedicated to protecting critical digital infrastructures through innovative and comprehensive security solutions. Our team of experts provides cutting-edge services to safeguard against cyber threats, ensuring the security and resilience of our clients’ operations.
