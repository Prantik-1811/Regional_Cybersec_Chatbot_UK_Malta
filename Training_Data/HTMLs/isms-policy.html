It is the policy of Reliance Cyber to pursue the following objectives; Establish and maintain an Information Security Management System that is compliant with ISO27001 Establish and maintain an information Security Management System that is compliant with Cyber Essentials and Cyber Essentials + Compliance to all applicable laws and regulations and contractual obligations Provision of Information Security education to all staff Implementation of Information Security Objectives that are established following the results of risk assessments This policy sets a framework for continuous improvement which is reviewed annually by Reliance Cyber leadership Team to ensure it remains appropriate and suitable to the business. Responsibility for upholding this policy company-wide is under the authority of the Chief Information Security Officer (CISO) with the expectation of personal commitment of all staff to address information security as part of their skills.
