This service addresses growing cybersecurity challenges, skills shortages, and the lack of technical expertise needed to deploy and manage comprehensive zero-trust security solutions effectively. Managed SASE from Reliance Cyber simplifies the convergence of network and cybersecurity management by leveraging its extensive expertise in network security operations and industry-leading Managed Detection and Response (MDR). The service operationalises the Cato SASE Cloud Platform, known for its global private backbone with over 85 Points of Presence (PoPs), optimised SD-WAN, and advanced Layer 7 segmentation, to ensure secure, optimised, and reliable connectivity. Alex Martin, Cyber Services Director at Reliance Cyber, said, “Businesses today face increasing demands from hybrid working environments, complex multi-cloud and multi-vendor infrastructures, and evolving security threats. Our Managed SASE solution operationalises the powerful capabilities of Cato Networks, providing organisations with a simplified and unified approach to secure connectivity. We also believe that our MDR experience will allow us to enhance the Cato Networks offering, with additional threat intelligence and response features.” Key benefits of Reliance Cyber’s Managed SASE include: Simplified deployment and ongoing management of the Cato SASE Cloud Platform Reduced cybersecurity labour costs through outsourced management of network infrastructure and security operations. Enhanced performance and reduced latency leveraging Cato Networks’ global private backbone. Robust security policies enforced by dedicated cybersecurity experts. Comprehensive zero-trust security and secure remote access for hybrid and remote workforces. Improved threat visibility and rapid threat response capabilities via Reliance Cyber’s advanced MDR platform. Mark Gascoigne, Managing Director at Reliance Cyber, added, “Our Managed SASE offering bridges the gap for organisations confronting cybersecurity skill shortages and digital transformation pressures. Managed SASE from Reliance Cyber simplifies the convergence of network and cybersecurity management by leveraging its extensive expertise in network security operations and industry-leading Managed Detection and Response (MDR). The service operationalises the Cato SASE Cloud Platform, known for its global private backbone with over 85 Points of Presence (PoPs), optimised SD-WAN, and advanced Layer 7 segmentation, to ensure secure, optimised, and reliable connectivity.
