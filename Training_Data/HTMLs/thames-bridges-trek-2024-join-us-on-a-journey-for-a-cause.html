Giulia Foss - 12 August 2024 Estimated reading time: 2 minutes From Putney Bridge to Tower Bridge – 25 km & 16 Bridges. The Reliance Cyber team is gearing up for an exciting challenge as we join 2500 walkers on Saturday, 14th of September for the Thames Bridges Trek – Ultra Challenge . This event takes us across the heart of London, starting from Putney Bridge and ending at Southwark Park, spanning 25 kilometres and crossing 16 historic bridges. Along the way, we will be treated to unparalleled views of the city skyline and numerous historic landmarks. Why we trek This year, we are walking with a purpose. Our chosen charity is the Fountain Centre , an independent organisation located within St Luke’s Cancer Centre at the Royal Surrey Hospital. The Fountain Centre provides emotional and holistic support, information, and guidance for families affected by cancer. This cause is especially close to our hearts as they are currently supporting one of our colleagues and their family. Support our cause We invite you to support our journey and help us make a difference by contributing to our fundraising efforts. All proceeds will go directly to the Fountain Centre to help them continue their invaluable work. To donate, please visit our JustGiving page . Join us in making this trek more than just a walk across London – let’s make it a stride towards hope and support for those in need. CLICK HERE to visit our JustGiving page and donate.
