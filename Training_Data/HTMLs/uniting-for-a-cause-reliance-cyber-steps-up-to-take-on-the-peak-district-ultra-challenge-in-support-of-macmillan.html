Giulia Foss - 20 December 2023 Estimated reading time: 2 minutes Contents Taking on the Challenge Fundraising for Macmillan Cancer Support Join Us in Making a Difference Reliance Cyber is excited to announce our participation in the upcoming Peak District Ultra Challenge fundraising event. On the 8th of July 2023, members of the Reliance Cyber team will be lacing up their walking shoes and taking on the challenge in Bakewell, Derbyshire. Through this endeavour, we aim to make a difference and contribute to the incredible work done by Macmillan Cancer Support . Taking on the Challenge Training schedules have been set, and our Reliance Cyber ramblers are hitting the trails and building their stamina in preparation to take on the 25k and 50k walking trails which will test their endurance, determination, and resilience. Fundraising for Macmillan Cancer Support While the Peak District Ultra Challenge presents a personal test for our team, it is the opportunity to contribute to Macmillan Cancer Support that truly motivates us. We have set up a fundraising page on JustGiving.com , where individuals and organisations can donate and support our cause. Every contribution, regardless of the amount, directly benefits Macmillan’s vital services, providing support, care, and assistance to those affected by cancer. Join Us in Making a Difference We invite all our valued clients, partners, and community members to join us in making a difference. Whether you choose to donate to our JustGiving page or share our story on social media, your support will help us achieve our fundraising goal. Together, we can demonstrate the power of collective action and make a significant impact on the lives of cancer patients and their families. Reliance Cyber is proud to support Macmillan Cancer Support through our participation in the Peak District Ultra Challenge. By coming together as a community, we can raise awareness and vital funds to help Macmillan continue their essential work. Join us in this worthy cause, and let’s make a lasting impact in the lives of those affected by cancer.
