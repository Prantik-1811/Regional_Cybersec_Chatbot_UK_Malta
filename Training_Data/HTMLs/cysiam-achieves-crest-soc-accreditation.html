Along with our Incident Response Team's achievement of NCSC CIR Level 2 and Full Member status with the Forum for Incident Response and Security Teams (FIRST), this adds a new dimension of credibility and trust for our current and prospective clients, as well as the CYSIAM team itself. We are over the moon with our most recent award and look forward to helping many more organisations to operate securely with confidence, thriving in the digital age. CYSIAM is a UK-based, 24x7x365 MDR security partner providing protection against, detection of, and response to cyber attacks. All CYSIAM staff are HMG security-vetted and we are one of the only FIRST UK Full Member SOCs, highlighting our integrated and threat-led Incident Response capability. Since 2006 CREST has been leading the cyber security community to collectively raise the standards of cyber service providers and professionals, quality assuring the sector and in turn providing confidence to the buying community, government and regulators. CREST has over 370 accredited member companies against a rigorous standard, operating across dozens of countries. They also certify thousands of professionals worldwide putting them through their paces in industry leading examinations. CREST collaborates with governments, regulators, academia, training partners, professional bodies and other stakeholders around the world.
