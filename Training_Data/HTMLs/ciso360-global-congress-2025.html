As a Sapphire Sentinel Sponsor, Reliance Cyber will play a prominent role at one of Europe’s most respected cybersecurity leadership forums. The event will bring together 100–150 CISOs and senior security practitioners from across Finance, Technology, Professional Services, and other critical sectors. Attendees will take part in keynotes, panel sessions, roundtables, and cultural networking activities, all under the Chatham House Rule to promote open and candid discussion. As part of the sponsorship, Alex Martin, Director of Managed Services at Reliance Cyber , will speak at the congress, joining a line-up of global cybersecurity leaders to share practical insights and front-line perspectives on resilience, detection, and response in a volatile threat landscape. “CISO 360 is a rare opportunity to hear directly from those leading security strategy in global organisations,” said Alex Martin, Director of Managed Services. Building on its Google-powered threat intelligence capabilities, Reliance Cyber will also be offering follow-up opportunities for CISOs to take part in bespoke Threat Modelling Workshops—designed to help security leaders map risk, validate controls, and prioritise investment based on real-world attack paths. A limited number of complimentary passes are available for senior in-house security practitioners. London, UK – 30 th May 2025 – Reliance Cyber is proud to announce its sponsorship of the Pulse 9th CISO 360 Global Congress , taking place from 24–26 June 2025 at the prestigious Bayerischer Hof Hotel in Munich, Germany. As a Sapphire Sentinel Sponsor, Reliance Cyber will play a prominent role at one of Europe’s most respected cybersecurity leadership forums. The event will bring together 100–150 CISOs and senior security practitioners from across Finance, Technology, Professional Services, and other critical sectors. Attendees will take part in keynotes, panel sessions, roundtables, and cultural networking activities, all under the Chatham House Rule to promote open and candid discussion. As part of the sponsorship, Alex Martin, Director of Managed Services at Reliance Cyber , will speak at the congress, joining a line-up of global cybersecurity leaders to share practical insights and front-line perspectives on resilience, detection, and response in a volatile threat landscape. “CISO 360 is a rare opportunity to hear directly from those leading security strategy in global organisations,” said Alex Martin, Director of Managed Services.
