The new partnership will see Reliance Cyber working closely with Maintel to develop a detailed understanding of customers’ risk profiles, creating risk aligned bespoke solutions that counter evolving cyber security threats and protect customers’ critical assets. As part of the partnership, Reliance Cyber will provide a combination of services to Maintel’s customers including: Extended Detection and Response (XDR) Virtual Chief Information Security Office (vCISO) Cyber Security Maturity Assessment Dan Davies CTO, Maintel commented: “With cyber security attacks on the rise and increasing in sophistication, Maintel is continually looking at ways and means of countering these evolving threats for our customers. This new partnership with Reliance Cyber builds on Maintel’s existing and strong capabilities around network security managed services by adding wider cyber security offerings to our portfolio, signifying our continuing commitment to helping safeguard the security of our customers.” Joining forces with Reliance Cyber will enable Maintel to ramp up its security service portfolio and provide a diverse range of professional and managed services, ultimately helping customers overcome their security challenges and focus on their wider business and strategic goals. Maintel will also have the capability to provide an incident response service to support customers in the advent of a security breach. The service is designed to equip customers with incident response plans, providing advice on how to react appropriately if an incident occurs, ultimately ensuring that customers are as resilient as possible against such attacks. Mark Gascoigne, Managing Director of Reliance Cyber said: “In an increasingly complex threat landscape, we are seeing businesses face greater challenges in relation to cyber security. Many organisations are struggling with preparedness, something which is often compounded by a lack of dedicated resource and in-house skills. Gascoigne went on to say “Across our various markets, we’ve seen an opportunity to answer this need by delivering more agile and robust cyber security solutions for our partners. We look forward to helping Maintel strengthen their security offering to their customers further, with our bespoke solutions.” About Maintel Maintel is a cloud and managed services company with a focus on communications. Its people are experienced, trusted technology advisors, who improve their clients’ organisations through digital transformation. Founded in 1991, Maintel has a proven track record of delivering communications solutions and services to both the private and public sector. In that time, the company has gained the expertise to solve the challenges associated with digital initiatives, and its managed services approach ensures clients realise the full value of their technology. This is achieved by specialising in three key pillars of digital transformation: making organisations more effective, efficient, and collaborative with digital workplace solutions; allowing organisations to acquire, develop, and retain more business by using technology to create more meaningful customer experiences through outstanding service; ensuring organisations’ employees, customers, and partners always have seamless access to their data and applications through reliable, secure connectivity. Ongoing growth of Maintel’s cloud and managed services offerings continues to allow the company to keep supporting the increasing demands of the market. Please visit www.maintel.co.uk for more information. About Reliance Cyber At Reliance Cyber, we believe in truly partnering with our customers. We work with organisations across multiple sectors, to defend them against advanced cyber threats including up to nation-state-level. We develop a real, in-depth understanding of the risks an organisation faces and creating bespoke solutions in line with global best practice. Our industry-leading cyber security expertise and experience, coupled with best-of-breed toolsets, enable, us to protect our customers’ most important assets, allowing them to focus on the things that they do best. Please visit www.reliancecyber.com for further information.
