https://www.cybercrowd.co.uk/news/cybercrowd-wins-employer-of-the-year-at-thames-valley-chamber-of-commerce-business-awards-2024 News Reading, Berkshire – July 27, 2024 – CyberCrowd is thrilled to announce its win as ‘Employer of the Year’ at the Thames Valley Chamber of Commerce Business Awards 2024. The gala event, held in Reading, Berkshire, was a celebration of excellence and innovation in business, expertly hosted by Brandon Cole and the Thames Valley Chamber of Commerce. “We’re delighted to receive the Employer of the Year award at the Thames Valley Chamber of Commerce Business Awards 2024! This achievement reflects the dedication of our exceptional team at CyberCrowd, whose hard work and commitment are central to our ability to deliver outstanding services to our clients. The evening was a fantastic celebration, and collecting this award was a true honour for us,” stated Mike Robinson, Managing Director at CyberCrowd. CyberCrowd’s win underscores its dedication to fostering a supportive and growth-oriented workplace culture. The company invests in its team through comprehensive apprenticeship programs and ongoing training with top organisations such as CREST, NCSC, and The Cyber Scheme. These efforts not only empower individuals but also strengthen CyberCrowd’s position as a leader in the cybersecurity industry. “We are committed to investing in our people, from our apprenticeship programs that kickstart careers to ongoing training with top organisations. These efforts not only boost individual growth but also strengthen our business,” added Mike. CyberCrowd thanks the event sponsors, especially IBB Law for sponsoring the Employer of the Year category, and special thanks to the Thames Valley Chamber of Commerce for putting together such a fantastic event. Congratulations to all the other award winners and those shortlisted. The Thames Valley has an incredible business community, and CyberCrowd is proud to be part of it.
