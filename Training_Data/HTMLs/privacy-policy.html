How long will cookies remain on my device? The length of time cookies will stay on your device will depend on whether it is a “persistent” or “session” cookie. A persistent cookie will be stored by a web browser and will remain valid until its set expiration date (typically after 1 year, but this is cookie specific), unless deleted by you before the expiration date. A session cookie, on the other hand, will expire at the end of your web session, when the web browser is closed. 4. How can I control cookies? You can control and manage cookies in various ways.
