CyberCrowd’s Data Protection Gap Analysis gives you a clear, detailed picture of how your organisation measures up against the UK GDPR and the Data Protection Act 2018. Whether you’re building from scratch or strengthening existing frameworks, our tailored approach uncovers shortfalls, identifies opportunities for improvement, and sets out actionable steps to move forward. We go beyond surface-level checks. Our consultants engage directly with your key stakeholders through focused interviews and questionnaires to gain a meaningful understanding of your current data protection posture – from policies and privacy notices to real-world practices across departments.
