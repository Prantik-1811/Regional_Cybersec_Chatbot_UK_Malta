As Reliance Cyber and Google embark on this journey, both organisations are committed to setting new standards in cyber security and delivering strategic benefits to customers. This piece tackles the subject of ingestion based Security information and event management (SIEM), providing deep insights into the challenges and opportunities presented by SIEM platforms. Ultimately discussing the importance of focusing on the value of data over its volume, optimising security investments, and employing strategic threat modelling to enhance security responses.
