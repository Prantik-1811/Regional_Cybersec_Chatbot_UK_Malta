Enter the Managed Security Operations Centre (SOC) – your frontline defence for 24/7 threat detection, response, and remediation. What is a Managed SOC? A Managed SOC is a service that combines cutting-edge technology and skilled cybersecurity analysts to monitor, detect, and respond to threats in real-time. By analysing activity across your network, devices, applications, and data, a SOC identifies vulnerabilities and incidents, enabling proactive protection against cyberattacks. The backbone of a SOC is the SIEM (Security Information and Event Management) tool, which provides continuous monitoring of your IT environment. It establishes a baseline for what “normal” looks like, flagging any anomalies that could signal potential threats. Why Does Your Organisation Need a SOC? Today’s complex IT environments – with hybrid working, cloud integrations, and personal devices – make it challenging to maintain visibility and control. Cyber threats are becoming more sophisticated, and the stakes have never been higher. A Managed SOC provides: 24/7 Monitoring : Round-the-clock vigilance ensures threats are detected and acted upon, even when your team is offline.
