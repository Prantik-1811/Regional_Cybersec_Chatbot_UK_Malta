Cyber Essentials Plus (CE Plus): Builds on the CE framework but involves a hands-on technical assessment by a certified expert to ensure compliance with security controls. This process involves a detailed audit of your cybersecurity measures, such as malware protection, patch management, and network vulnerabilities.
