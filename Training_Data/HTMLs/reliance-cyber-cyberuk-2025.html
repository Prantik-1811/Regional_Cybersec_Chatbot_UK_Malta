Plan your visit We are excited to announce that Reliance Cyber will be exhibiting at CYBERUK 2025 , taking place from 6–8 May at Manchester Central . Hosted by the UK’s National Cyber Security Centre (NCSC) , CYBERUK is the UK’s flagship cybersecurity event, bringing together more than 2,000 senior decision-makers, technologists, and industry experts from across the public and private sectors. This year’s theme, “Transforming Resilience. Countering Threats,” reflects the critical need for organisations to build stronger, faster and more adaptive defences against an evolving threat landscape. Where to find us You will find us at Stand E27 , where we will be showcasing how Reliance Cyber helps organisations improve security outcomes through automation, advanced threat detection and expert partnership. Our team will be available throughout the event to discuss how we support organisations to: Reduce MSS onboarding times to under 30 minutes Drive faster mean time to detect (MTTD) and mean time to respond (MTTR) Enhance resilience across complex IT and OT environments Book time for a free consultation We are offering visitors the opportunity to book time with our team during the event to discuss their current security questions, concerns, or challenges. Our CTO and Director of Managed Services will be available on the stand to provide expert guidance and free consultations. Whether you want to explore ways to strengthen your resilience, optimise your detection and response, or simply sense-check your existing security approach, we are here to help. If you would like to arrange a specific time in advance, please use the meeting scheduler link to book: https://landing.reliancecyber.com/meetings/giulia-foss — or simply stop by Stand E27 during the show. Interactive racing car simulator on the stand On the stand, we are bringing cybersecurity outcomes to life with an interactive racing car simulator . Just as speed and precision are vital on the track, they are critical in cybersecurity. Live cemonstrations and key insights We will also be featuring showcasing: A live demo of our Managed Detection and Response (MDR) service Key insights around our strategic partnership with Google and how it enhances client resilience Key differentiators in our approach to managed security and professional services Why visit Reliance Cyber at CYBERUK? At a time when cyber threats are becoming increasingly sophisticated, we help organisations build resilience by combining the best of automation, threat intelligence and expert human insight. Whether you are looking to optimise detection and response, improve onboarding efficiency, or strengthen your security architecture, our team will be on hand to offer advice, insights, and practical solutions. Plan your visit Dates : 6–8 May 2025 Venue : Manchester Central, Windmill Street, Manchester, M2 3GX Stand : E27 If you are attending CYBERUK 2025, we would love to meet you. Stop by Stand E27 to experience our racing simulator, watch live demonstrations, and discover how Reliance Cyber is helping organisations transform their resilience and counter today’s cyber threats. We look forward to seeing you in Manchester.
