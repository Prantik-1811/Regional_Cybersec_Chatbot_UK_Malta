https://www.cybercrowd.co.uk/news/cybercrowd-awarded-crest-soc-accreditation News We’re proud to announce that CyberCrowd has been awarded the CREST Security Operations Centre (SOC) Accreditation , further strengthening our commitment to delivering secure, high-quality, and resilient cyber security services through our UK-based, 24/7/365 SOC. This prestigious accreditation recognises that CyberCrowd’s SOC meets the rigorous standards set by CREST , the internationally recognised body representing the highest levels of assurance and professionalism in the cyber security industry. By adding the SOC discipline to our CREST membership , we continue to invest in robust, accountable, and independently assessed services that matter most to our customers. What the CREST SOC Accreditation Means CREST’s SOC accreditation is more than a badge – it’s a framework of excellence . It requires a comprehensive, independent assessment of our governance, operational processes, people, and technology. This ensures that our security operations are not just operationally effective, but built on strong, auditable foundations that can stand up to the ever-evolving threat landscape. For our customers, this means confidence that our SOC is operating at the highest possible standard – with mature threat detection, response, and continuous improvement processes in place. This achievement complements our wider offering, which includes: An NCSC-Certified Incident Response Team Recognition as an NCSC Cyber Incident Exercise Assured Service Provider Together, these accreditations reflect our end-to-end capability to help organisations prepare for, defend against, and respond to cyber threats . Today’s SOC Is a Strategic Business Enabler Security Operations Centres are no longer just technical backrooms focused solely on alerts and logs. In today’s world of rising compliance demands, tighter cyber insurance requirements, and a growing volume of sophisticated attacks, the SOC has become a strategic pillar of business resilience . A modern, integrated SOC like CyberCrowd’s delivers more than “eyes on glass.” It helps: Strengthen business continuity by detecting and responding to threats before they cause disruption. Simplify regulatory compliance by aligning with frameworks like ISO 27001, NIS2, and DORA. Reduce cyber insurance premiums by demonstrating proactive, auditable security controls. Provide real-time visibility through 24/7 threat monitoring, investigation, and response. Enable smarter decision-making through actionable intelligence and insights. A Commitment to Quality, Assured by Independent Oversight Our CREST SOC Accreditation is not just a milestone – it’s part of our ongoing mission to provide customers with trusted, independently validated cyber defence services . It represents the high bar we set for ourselves – and the standard we believe our customers should expect from their cyber security providers. We’re proud of this achievement, but more importantly, we’re proud of what it enables us to deliver to you: greater confidence, reduced risk, and always-on protection .
