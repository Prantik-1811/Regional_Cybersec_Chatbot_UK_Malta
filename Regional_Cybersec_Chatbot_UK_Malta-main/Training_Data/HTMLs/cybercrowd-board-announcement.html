https://www.cybercrowd.co.uk/news/cybercrowd-board-announcement News Serial Entrepreneur Join Cyber Security Board. Nicola Hartland, creator of industry leading data protection platform iCaaS, joins to complete the new board of ‘one-stop’ digital security firm; CyberCrowd. Following the company’s comprehensive overhaul under Chairman Trevor Rolls and Chief Executive Officer Mike Robinson, the now completed board has already begun work on its ambitious development strategy. Nicola Hartland CyberCrowd’s appointment for Chief Revenue Officer is charged with realising the board’s goal to achieve £10m revenue by 2025. Nicola’s background is in the creation, accelerated growth and sale of companies across a broad sector including GDPR & data security, sales outsourcing and her latest ethical supply chain venture; Just Sanitise, which was launched during Covid and quickly became a major NHS supplier as well as appearing on the shelves of over 300 supermarkets and across seven European countries. The company is a market leader in the digital security realm, offering everything from assessment services through to certification readiness; CISO, Pen Testing and DPO. Chief Executive Mike Robinson commented; “The skills, drive and experience that Nicola possess will complete our board and ensure the rapid and robust growth we have determined on, as we place CyberCrowd at the vanguard of this vital and ever-expanding sector. We are delighted to have secured their involvement” Nicola Hartland responded; “CyberCrowd is a thrilling new challenge for me. Digital security is critical to the safety and success of any organisation or company in an increasingly cyber-savvy world.
