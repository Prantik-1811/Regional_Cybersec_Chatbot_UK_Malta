Privacy Policy | CYSIAM Standard Terms of Business | Sustainability and Environmental Policy CYSIAM teams up with Cranfield University – Operational Digital Forensics CYSIAM teams up with Cranfield Defence and Security to deliver Operational Digital Forensics training to global clients Cranfield Defence and Security Services Ltd., a commercial trading entity of Cranfield University, has formed a new partnership with CYSIAM, a specialist cyber security provider. The two companies have signed a Collaboration Agreement to deliver courses at Cranfield University’s new state-of-the-art Digital Forensic facility near Milton Keynes. This training is aimed at global law enforcement, military and security services focusing on the technical and procedural skills required to conduct operational forensics and investigations on all types of digital platforms, from mobile phones to networks to drones. All training will be led by expert practitioners who have conducted digital forensics countering organised crime and terrorist operations under extreme pressure in some of the most hostile and challenging parts of the world. Underpinned by the most up-to-date research from Cranfield Defence and Security, these practical courses will enable delegates to develop sustainable skills to take back into their critical roles. Simon Harwood, Managing Director of Cranfield Defence and Security Services Ltd, commented “Digital Forensics is an ‘in-demand’ skill and we are delighted to partner with CYSIAM to bring operational experience to our portfolio of defence and security education and research.” Steve Lancaster, CYSIAM CEO, added “We feel privileged to be working alongside an organisation with the history and global respect that Cranfield has. Our focus is on ensuring that people who protect and support freedom around the globe are able to capture and secure digital evidence of wrongdoing, enabling safe and fair judicial prosecutions.” If you are interested in discussing Digital Forensic training requirements for your organisation(s) please send email to either infocdssl@cdssl.co.uk at Cranfield Defence and Security or info@cysiam.com at CYSIAM. About Cranfield Defence and Security Services Limited As a specialist postgraduate university, Cranfield University’s world-class expertise, large-scale facilities and unrivalled industry partnerships are creating leaders in technology and management globally. Cranfield Defence and Security Services Ltd supports and expands the University’s defence and security relationships in new, agile, and flexible, commercial directions. Our main business activities are consultancy, symposia, test and evaluation, and training. About CYSIAM Limited CYSIAM provides world leading expertise in offensive security and critical incident response. Through our technology-enabled services, we secure freedom for organisations across the globe enabling them to operate and evolve without fear. Using our government and military cyber expertise we train our clients to be able to protect themselves and respond to attacks and breaches when they occur. Our team share a fundamental desire to help people protect themselves, to give them the security, operational resilience and freedom to prosper in the digital age.
