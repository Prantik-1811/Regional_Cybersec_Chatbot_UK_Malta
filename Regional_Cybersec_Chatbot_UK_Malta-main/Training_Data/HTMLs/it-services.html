Managed service providers (MSPs) are given access to the intimate details of an organisation, their systems, software, and data, it is important to ensure that your security and compliance is up to date and fit for purpose. Set out in The Network Information Security (NIS) Regulations 2018, the government is bringing MSPs into scope to help better protect the UKs critical national infrastructure. If you are an MSP providing services to the public sector you will be expected to comply with these regulations. Although the regulations do not yet cover the work of MSPs in the private sector, these organisations are still looking for a certain level of assurance that the intimate data an MSP holds on them, and their systems is being properly protected. Non-compliance can face potential fines of up to £17 million ($20 million).
